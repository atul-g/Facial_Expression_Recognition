Now Capturing Video Through Webcam


Look at the webcam of your laptop and face it in a frontal manner. Don't cover your face using your hand as it will have difficulty detecting your face then. Although OpenCV can detect the face even in a dark lit room, it is better to be in a well lit room to get a beter prediction result of the expressions.
